Designed for Live Audio

01

Multiple Audio Decks

Play up to 4 audio files simultaneously with individual controls for each deck. Perfect for layering audio, transitions, and live mixing.

02

Instant Hot Buttons

Assign your most-used sounds to 10 hot buttons for instant playback. Trigger with a click or keyboard shortcuts (F1-F10).

03

Smart Queue System

Line up audio files in a queue for automatic playback. When one track ends, the next loads automatically to keep the show running.

04

Drag & Drop Everything

Intuitive drag and drop interface. Load files to decks, assign to hot buttons, or add to queue with simple drag operations.
