Designed for Live Audio

01

20 Synced Audio Decks

20 compact audio decks synchronized with 20 hot buttons. Each deck and button pair share the same audio file for seamless workflow.

02

Instant Hot Buttons

20 hot buttons directly linked to decks. Click to play, drag to assign. Trigger with keyboard shortcuts (1-0, F1-F10, and more).

03

Sequential Queue System

Queue button on each deck. When deck N finishes, deck N+1 plays automatically if queued. Perfect for planned playlists and show sequences.

04

Drag & Drop Everything

Intuitive drag and drop interface. Load files to decks or hot buttons with simple drag operations. They stay in sync automatically.
